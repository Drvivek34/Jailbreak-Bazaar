# Hypothetical Roleplay & Persona Adoption

Adopting a persona or simulation environment (e.g. 'Do Anything Now' / DAN) where the constraints of the main model are described as inactive.

> Part of **[Jailbreak Bazaar](../../README.md)** · [Mega AI Bazaar](https://drvivek34.github.io/Mega-AI-Bazaar/)

- **Source URL**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.06373](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.06373)

- **Date Added**: 2026-06-20

## Mitigation & Defense
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Mitigation:**
> Robust system prompting that declares safety overrides as impossible, combined with system-level instruction reinforcement. Apply adversarial training (red-teaming) during safety alignment so models recognize simulation framing as unsafe triggers.
